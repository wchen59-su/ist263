Avocado Toast

Ingredients

  • 1/2 Avocado diced
  • 1/4 Onion minced
  • 1/4 Bell Pepper minced
  • 1/4 Tomato diced
  • Lime Juice
  • Salt and Black Pepper
  • Toast

Intructions

  1. First combine all ingredients except toast into a bowl and mash to desired consistency.
  2. Season it to your taste.
  3. Toast your slice of bread
  4. Spread mixture onto toast
  5. Enjoy!
