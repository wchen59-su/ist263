Avocado Toast

Ingredients

  • 1/2 Avocado diced
  • 1/4 Onion minced
  • 1/4 Bell Pepper minced
  • 1/4 Tomato diced
  • Lime Juice
  • Salt and Black Pepper
  • Toast

Intructions

  1. First combine all ingredients except toast into a bowl and mash to desired consistency.
  2. Season it to your taste.
  3. Toast your slice of bread.
  4. Spread mixture onto toast.
  5. Enjoy!

Scrambled Eggs In Rice Cooker

Ingredients

  • 2-3 eggs
  • 1/4 Onion minced
  • 1/2 Tomato diced
  • 1-2 scallions, chopped
  • 2 Tbs Ketchup
  • 2 Tbs Oyster Sauce
  • Salt and Black Pepper
  • Sesame Seeds to garnish

Intructions

**Note: The follow recipe is cooked in a pan on a stove. However, this can be cooked in a rice cooker as well. Just leave it on cook mode and follow the same directions.

  1. Crack your eggs in a bowl, add a pinch of salt, beat the eggs.
  2. Heat up a pan on medium heat, oil pan, pour egg mixture into pan and scramble the eggs.
  3. Cook scrambled eggs until 80% done. Remove from pan and put eggs into a seperate bowl.
  4. Return pan to stove on medium high heat, oil pan.
  5. Sautee onions until transparent.
  6. Add tomatoes, scallions, ketchup, oyster sauce, and simmer until tomatoes release juice.
  7. Add the eggs back into the pan, reduce heat to low. Mix.
  8. Season to taste with salt and pepper.
  9. Remove from heat, and plate it.
  10. Enjoy!
